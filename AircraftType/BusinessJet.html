About Business Jets

Business jets are aircraft designed for transporting small groups of people in comfort and style. They range from light jets perfect for regional travel to ultra-long-range aircraft capable of intercontinental flights. Business jets offer flexibility, privacy, time savings, and access to smaller airports unavailable to commercial airlines.
