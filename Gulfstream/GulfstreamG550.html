Gulfstream G550

The Gulfstream G550, manufactured from 2003-2021, requires a 2 person crew and can transport up to 18 passengers. The aircraft has a maximum operating altitude of 15,545 m (51,000 ft), a normal cruise speed of 904 km/h (488 KTS), and a 12,501 km (6,750 NM) seats-full range.
