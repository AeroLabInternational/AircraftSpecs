ガルフストリーム G550 スペックシート

The Gulfstream G550, manufactured from 2003-2021, requires a 2 person crew and can transport up to 18 passengers. The aircraft has a maximum operating altitude of 51,000 ft, a normal cruise speed of 488 KTS / 561 MPH, and a 6,750 NM seats-full range.
